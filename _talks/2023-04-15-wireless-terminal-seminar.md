Dr. Jianfeng Qian was invited to deliver an **Invited Talk** at the *2023 Seminar on New-Generation Wireless Terminal Technologies*,  
organized in a hybrid format (online and onsite) in April 2023.  

The talk summarized his research on **advanced terminal antenna design**,  
including filtering and decoupling techniques for next-generation wireless communication systems.  

**Host:** National university research team (China)  
**Date:** April 15, 2023  
**Type:** Invited presentation (20 min, no paper or registration required)
