    Synthesis and Design of Transversal Filtering Antennas

    Published in IEEE Transactions on Antennas and Propagation, 2025

    This paper proposes the first systematic synthesis framework for transversal filtering antennas (TFAs), bridging classical coupling-matrix filter synthesis with radiating structures.
    It enables precise control of radiation transmission zeros, achieving both in-band selectivity and out-of-band suppression.

    Key Contributions

    • Established the theoretical foundation for transversal filtering antennas.
    • Developed synthesis procedures linking coupling matrices to antenna geometries.
    • Verified the theory through full-wave simulation and measurement, forming a closed-loop validation.
